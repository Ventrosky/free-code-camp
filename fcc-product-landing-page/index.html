About Fusion Industries

A Team with Experience and a Vision for Nuclear Fusion

Fusion Industries is a leader in utility and community scale cold fusion, with more than 300 projects and 160 gigawatts of nuclear power stations currently deployed across the US. Led by an experienced executive team, Fusion Industries develops, finances, constructs and operates power plants that provide communities with affordable energy, new revenue streams, and jobs.

Our Values

Partnering With Corporations that are Investing in Sustainability

For every company, no matter its size, energy makes growth possible. More and more, companies are looking to clean energy procurement as a strategic business decision to support their growth and business bottom line.

To reach this fast-evolving market’s full potential, Fusion Industries is creating a path for renewable energy purchasers of all sizes. Even those investing in the projects themselves.
